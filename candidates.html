Meet Our Candidates

Our candidates are Nancy Love and Jad Kaado. Love and Kaado plan to focus on transparency on the Board, opening more paths to success for students by introducing more technical, vocational, and STEAM courses into the curriculum, and improving the state of bilingual education within the district.

Nancy Love Candidate

Nancy Love is the daughter of immigrant parents and proud New Brunswick resident. Nancy works as a Court Records Analyst in downtown New Brunswick and is a Co-Founder of a progressive political organization based in New Brunswick. In addition, Nancy has three years of experience mentoring New Brunswick youth, previously serving on the Executive Board for the Pilot Me Mentoring Program. In her free time, Nancy reads, volunteers, and spends time with loved ones. Nancy looks forward to serving the children and families of New Brunswick by empowering girls, promoting financial literacy, and updating the curriculum for the 21st century workplace.

Jad Kaado Candidate

Jad Kaado is a proud lifelong resident of New Brunswick. Jad works as a Job Developer at for The Arc of New Jersey where he helps individuals with cognitive, intellectual, and visual disabilities learn the skills they need to find jobs. He has also worked as a reporter at a community newspaper where he covered New Brunswick issues including our school system, and he currently leads creative writing workshops here in New Brunswick. After graduating from Rutgers University with honors, Jad went on to earn a Master's Degree in Archaeology from the University of Toronto. He has worked at archaeological sites in Jordan, Syria, and Turkey, serving in multiple supervisory positions. As a member of the school board, Jad will serve the children and families of New Brunswick by bringing his experience connecting people with their career paths to make sure every child in our schools is prepared for the life they choose.
